Project title: Mess Management System

ðŸ§¾ The purpose of this Mess Management System is to streamline and automate the day-to-day operations of a food delivery or mess service. This project aims to simplify the management of employees, customers, food categories, products (meals), and orders using a centralized application. It reduces manual work, minimizes errors, ensures organized data handling, and enhances operational efficiency for both the admin and employees.The system helps to improve customer service, speed up order processing, and maintain a digital log of all operations.

    Activities
  1. 1. View Menu
    1. Customers can browse the daily or upcoming menus without logging in.
    2. Display item descriptions, pricing, and any special notes for the day.
  2. 2. Preview Meal Options
    1. Potential customers can view and explore different meal options available, such as breakfast, lunch, and dinner.
    2. Visual representations of meals (e.g., photos or icons) to increase appeal.
  3. 3. Contact Mess Staff or Admin
    1. Provide an option to directly contact the mess admin or customer support for inquiries without needing an account.
    2. Contact options can include email, phone number, or a simple message form.
  4. 4. About Us Section
    1. Display information about the mess service, the team, and its history to familiarize new customers.
    2. Highlight the mess's unique qualities, such as food quality, variety, or delivery efficiency.
  5. 5. Social Media Links and Updates
    1. Integrate links to social media platforms for customers to follow updates or engage with the community.
    2. Regularly updated feeds for events, special menus, or seasonal changes.
  6. 6. Food Quality Assurance Information
    1. Include information on food sourcing, hygiene standards, and preparation practices.
    2. Showcase certifications or quality assurances if applicable.

Project title: On Medical Services

ðŸ§¾The purpose of this Medical Services Project is to provide patients with an efficient, user-friendly, and secure platform to access healthcare services remotely. In many regions, accessing timely medical care can be challenging due to long wait times, lack of specialist availability, or inefficient appointment systems. This project is designed to bridge that gap by digitizing the appointment process and integrating basic symptom-based disease prediction to help patients connect with the right doctors faster.

    Activities
  1. 1. Account Registration & Login
    1. Users can create a new, valid account and log in using secure credentials.
  2. 2. Specialist Search
    1. Patients can search for medical specialists based on their health condition or preference.
  3. 3. View Doctor List
    1. A categorized list of available doctors is displayed with their specialization, contact details, and availability.
  4. 4. Book Appointments
    1. Patients can book appointments with a selected doctor.
    2. During booking, patients must enter their symptoms to enable diagnosis.
  5. 5. Symptom-Based Prediction Support
    1. During the appointment booking process, the system uses entered symptoms to support early disease prediction (if applicable).
  6. 6. Provide Feedback
    1. After a consultation, patients can give feedback or rate their experience with the doctor.
  7. 7. Doctor Login & Profile Management
    1. Doctors must log in with valid credentials.
    2. Doctors must provide verified contact information (email and phone number) for consultations.
  8. 8. Appointment Management
    1. Doctors can view and manage their daily appointments and schedules from the system dashboard.
